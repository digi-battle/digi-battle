Error 404 page on adultswim.com. I think 404 pages are a great place for creative freedom.

  1. because it can relieve the user's frustration at having their journey interrupted by an error
  2. there's not many other places on most websites that allow designers/developers to create something pointless and fun

Adult Swim opted to display an existential crisis from the voice of the website. Never seen something like this on an error page before.
